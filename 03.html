About Maluti School

Founded in 1952, Maluti School has been providing exceptional education for over 70 years. Our mission is to develop well-rounded individuals who are prepared to meet the challenges of the future with confidence and integrity.

We offer a comprehensive curriculum that balances academic excellence with character development, ensuring our students grow both intellectually and personally.

Our campus features state-of-the-art facilities, including modern science laboratories, a comprehensive library, sports fields, and dedicated arts spaces.

At Maluti, we believe in education with heart and soul - nurturing not just minds, but compassionate individuals who will make a positive impact on the world.

Our faculty consists of dedicated professionals who are experts in their fields and passionate about teaching. They provide personalized attention to each student, fostering a love for learning that lasts a lifetime.

Beyond the classroom, we offer numerous extracurricular activities including sports teams, music programs, debate clubs, and community service opportunities.

Maluti School graduates are known for their critical thinking skills, ethical leadership, and readiness for higher education and professional careers.

We welcome students from diverse backgrounds and believe that our inclusive community enriches the educational experience for everyone.

Whether you're a prospective student, parent, or educator, we invite you to explore what makes Maluti School a special place to learn and grow.
