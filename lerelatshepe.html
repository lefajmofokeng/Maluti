Lere La Tshepe Campus - 2026

Lere La Tshepe Campus

A center of excellence for Information Technology, Business Studies, and Hospitality Management. We are dedicated to equipping students with practical skills for the modern workforce.

Current State

Modernizing for the future of skills training.

Lere La Tshepe is currently undergoing an exciting phase of digital and infrastructural transformation. We have recently upgraded our three main computer laboratories with high-spec machines to support our NCV IT curriculum.


Our hospitality wing has received new industrial-grade kitchen equipment to ensure our NATED and NCV students train in environments that mirror top-tier hotels. The campus grounds are fully operational, safe, and vibrant with student life.

Academic Programmes (2026)

Information Technology

NCV Programme (National Certificate Vocational)

This vocational programme is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in the IT industry. Students will learn the building blocks of software development, how computers communicate in a network, and the intricacies of hardware maintenance. It combines theory with practical lab work to prepare students for technical roles immediately upon completion.

Level Duration Curriculum Subjects
Level 2 1 Year
  • Introduction to Systems Development
  • Electronics & Digital Concepts
  • Introduction to Information Systems
  • Mathematics / Mathematical Literacy
  • English First Additional Language
  • Life Orientation
Level 3 1 Year
  • Systems Analysis and Design
  • Computer Hardware and Software
  • Principles of Computer Programming
  • Mathematics
  • Life Orientation
Level 4 1 Year
  • Systems Development (Advanced Programming)
  • Data Communication and Networking
  • IT Project Management
  • Mathematics
  • Life Orientation

Admission Requirements

  • Grade 9 Pass (minimum)
  • Preference given to students with Mathematics aptitude
  • Placement test may be required

Career Opportunities

  • Junior Software Developer
  • IT Technician / Support Specialist
  • Network Administrator
  • Systems Analyst Assistant
  • Database Administrator

Business Studies

NATED Programme (Report 191)

The Business Studies NATED programme focuses on the administrative, financial, and managerial aspects of the modern business world. This course moves at a fast pace (semester based) and is ideal for students who want to enter the corporate world with a strong understanding of entrepreneurship, sales, and personnel management.

Certificate Duration Curriculum Subjects
N4 1 Semester
  • Entrepreneurship and Business Management N4
  • Management Communication N4
  • Computer Practice N4
  • Financial Accounting N4
N5 1 Semester
  • Sales Management N5
  • Labour Relations N5
  • Entrepreneurship and Business Management N5
  • Computer Practice N5
N6 1 Semester
  • Marketing Management N6
  • Personnel Management N6
  • Entrepreneurship and Business Management N6
  • Computer Practice N6

Admission Requirements

  • Grade 12 (NSC) or Senior Certificate
  • Or NCV Level 4 Certificate

Career Opportunities

  • Business Administrator
  • Sales Consultant / Manager
  • Human Resources Assistant
  • Entrepreneur / Small Business Owner
  • Marketing Assistant
Apply for Business Studies

Hospitality Management

Combined NCV & NATED Offerings

Hospitality Management at Lere La Tshepe covers everything from professional cookery and food preparation to front-of-house services and hotel management. Students train in industrial kitchens and learn the high standards of service required by the international tourism and hospitality sector.

Stream Level/Cert Curriculum Subjects
NCV
(Vocational)
1 Year per level		 Level 2 Food Preparation, Hospitality Services, Client Services & Human Relations, Life Orientation.
Level 3 Food Preparation, Hospitality Generics, Client Services, Math Literacy.
Level 4 Advanced Food Preparation, Hospitality Services, Professional Cookery, Math Literacy.
NATED
(Report 191)
1 Semester per cert		 N4 Applied Management N4, Catering Theory & Practical N4, Sanitation & Safety N4, Nutrition & Menu Planning N4.
N5 Applied Management N5, Catering Theory & Practical N5, Food & Beverage Service N5, Entrepreneurship N5.
N6 Applied Management N6, Catering Theory & Practical N6, Communication N6, Computer Practice N6.

Admission Requirements

  • NCV: Grade 9 Pass (min)
  • NATED: Grade 12 with Tourism/Consumer Studies preferred

Career Opportunities

  • Professional Chef / Sous Chef
  • Hotel Receptionist / Front Office Manager
  • Event & Catering Coordinator
  • Restaurant Manager
  • Guest House Manager
